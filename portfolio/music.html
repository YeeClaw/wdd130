Music has brought profound changes to my life in many different forms. It has allowed me to think about problems with a unique perspective, and have given me a greater appreciation for living in the moment. Additionally, it has been my best teacher for team work on just about any scale. Being able to work with a group of musicians is integral to any kind of performance.

Piano is my primary instrument of choice, and is what I have the most experience with. I can play mostly any genre and I'm great with working on the fly. I've played in jazz bands, and have even gigged with other pop/punk rock bands. Since I have started, I am trained both classically and with Jazz. This instrument is also my primary drive for the development of my music theory knowledge.

In efforts to diversify my music portfolio and expand my theory, I have also developed proficiencies with guitar. Much like with piano, jazz is my preferred route here, but I can also spice things up and play in really any band/ gig environment that is needed of me. I can play really any kind of guitar (although something of a steel guitar might be a challenge!) and style that is needed. This has also given me a little insight into the world of audio engineering and studio set ups.
